My Favorite Book:Fire And Blood

About the Book :

It tells the story of House Targaryen, a powerful family of dragonlords, nearly 200 years before the events of Game of Thrones.
The main conflict arises between Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen, the chosen heir of King Viserys I, and Prince Aegon II, his son from another marriage.
After Viserys I dies, both claim the throne, leading to a brutal war of succession.
The civil war causes dragons to turn against each other, leading to massive destruction and eventually the downfall of the Targaryen dragon-riding dominance.


Why I Love it

Epic Battles : Gritty, emotional, and beautifully shot.
Rich Lore : Expands the history of Westeros for longtime fans.
Dark Tone : Mature themes with high stakes and emotional depth.
Beautiful Cinematography : High production value with stunning visuals.
